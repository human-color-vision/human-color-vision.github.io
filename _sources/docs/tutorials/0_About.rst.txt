About this tutorial
===================

This is the official tutorial page for the paper **"A Computational Framework for Modeling the Emergence of Color Vision in the Human Brain"** by `Atsunobu Kotani <https://www.atsunobukotani.com/research>`_ and `Ren Ng <https://www2.eecs.berkeley.edu/Faculty/Homepages/yirenng.html>`_.


.. About this tutorial
.. -------------------

In this series of tutorials, we will guide you through the process of simulating the emergence of color vision in the human brain.

Once you finish the tutorials, you will be able to understand the basic structure of the codebase and how to modify it to suit your needs.
